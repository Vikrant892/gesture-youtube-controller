# Gesture Reference

Quick reference for all supported hand gestures.

| Gesture | Fingers | Action | YouTube Key |
|---------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Open Palm | All 5 up | Play / Pause | `Space` |
| Fist | All down | Mute / Unmute | `M` |
| Thumbs Up | Only thumb up | Volume Up | `Up Arrow` |
| Thumbs Down | Only thumb down | Volume Down | `Down Arrow` |
| Point (index) | Only index up | Skip Forward 5s | `Right Arrow` |
| Peace Sign | Index + Middle up | Toggle Fullscreen | `F` |

## Tips

- Hold your hand about 30-50cm from the camera for best results
- Make sure your hand is well lit (avoid backlighting)
- Keep gestures clear and distinct — half-extended fingers confuse the model
- There's a ~1 second cooldown between actions to prevent accidental repeats
- The thumb up/down detection needs your thumb clearly above or below your wrist
